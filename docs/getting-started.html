Getting Started

Learn how to make the most of your Disney+ Dual Subtitles experience.

🛠️ Quick Setup

1. Enable Extension

  • Open the extension popup by clicking the icon in your toolbar.
  • Ensure the "Enable Dual Subtitles" switch is turned ON.
  • Refresh your Disney+ page if you just installed it.

2. Select Languages

  • Open any video on Disney+.
  • Choose your Primary and Secondary languages.
  • Track Types: Choose Standard, [CC] (Accessibility), or [Forced] (Foreign parts only).

3. Custom Styling

  • Go to the "Styles" tab in the popup.
  • Adjust font size, color, and background transparency.
  • Changes are applied instantly to the active video!

4. Troubleshooting

  • If subtitles are out of sync, use [ and ] keys to adjust.
  • If subtitles stop appearing, try reloading the webpage.
  • For persistent issues, try restarting your browser.
💡 Smart Features

Intelligent Ranking: Your most used languages move to the top automatically!

Dynamic Hints: If a selected track (like Forced) has no content for the current scene, a hint will appear in the popup to let you know.
