Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This extension is designed to be fully local and data-safe.

✨ Data Collection

Zero Tracking

  • We do not collect any personal information.
  • No usage metrics or analytics are gathered.
  • We never sell or share your data.

Local Processing

  • All subtitle merging happens on your device.
  • Calculated offsets are never uploaded.
  • Your viewing history remains private.
🛠 Storage & Requests

Chrome Storage

Your settings (language preferences, custom styles) are stored using chrome.storage.sync solely to sync your preferences across your own devices.

Network Requests

The extension only makes requests to Disney+ servers to download subtitle fragments required for display. These requests contain no identifying information from our extension.

🚀 Open Source

This extension is open-source. You can audit the code at any time to verify our privacy claims.
