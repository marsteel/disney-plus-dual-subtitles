Privacy Policy

Disney+ Dual Subtitles is a Chrome & Edge extension committed to protecting your privacy.

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

✨ Overview

Disney+ Dual Subtitles is a browser extension designed to provide a premium dual-subtitle experience. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. We do not have servers.

📦 What We Store Locally

The following data is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.sync:

  • Language Settings: Selected primary and secondary languages.
  • Styling Preferences: Font color, size, background color, and weight settings.
  • Extension State: Enabled/disabled toggle status.

All settings are stored locally and synced across your devices via browser sync (if enabled). We never have access to this data.

📡 Third-Party Services

When you use Disney+ Dual Subtitles:

  • Subtitle fragments are downloaded directly from Disney+ servers to your browser.
  • We do not intercept, store, or have access to your viewing data or subtitle content.
  • No data is sent to any third-party AI or translation services.
🔧 Permissions Explained

storage

To save your language preferences and custom subtitle styles locally in your browser.

host_permissions

To access *.disneyplus.com to inject dual subtitles and fetch necessary subtitle segments.

🚀 Data Security & Open Source

All settings are stored using the browser's built-in storage. Since Disney+ Dual Subtitles is open source, you can review the complete source code on GitHub to verify our privacy claims at any time.
